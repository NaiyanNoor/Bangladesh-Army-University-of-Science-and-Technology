Message From VC

Vice Chancellor

Brig Gen A B M Humayun Kabir, ndc, psc, te

Bangladesh Army University of Science and Technology (BAUST) was founded in 2015 by Bangladesh Army with due permission from the Govt and UGC. Patronized by Bangladesh Army, the University aspires to transform itself into a centre of excellence in Science, Engineering and Technology by offering innovative multi-disciplinary courses and extensive research facility to the young generation of the country and beyond. The mission of BAUST is to provide a state-of-the-art education and research facility to develop Human Resources (HR) for the socio economic well being of the society
